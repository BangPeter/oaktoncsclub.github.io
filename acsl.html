Divisions

For all divisions, each contest consists of 5 short answer question, to be completed in 30 minutes, and 1 programming question which you have 72 hours to complete in any language.

You can choose which division you want to be part of -

  • Junior: for those looking into learning problem solving with computer science
  • Intermediate: currently taking APCS or have experience in coding.
  • Senior: taken APCS or equivalent, and are ready for a challenge.
